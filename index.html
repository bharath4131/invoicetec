⚖️ Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how InvoiceFlow collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our application.

1. Client-Side Data Storage

InvoiceFlow is designed as a privacy-first web application. All invoice documents, customer contact profiles, company information, and settings are stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB database. We do not transmit, sync, or back up your business data to external servers. Your financial data stays entirely on your machine unless you choose to export it manually.

2. Google AdSense & Third-Party Cookies

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements on our website to help fund the development of this tool. For AdSense to function properly:

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, users can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

3. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

4. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at our support address.
